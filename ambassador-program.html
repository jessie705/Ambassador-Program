Who Ambassadors Are

🤝
Culture Carriers

Not staff. Not pace leaders. Trusted, consistent members who model our values and make people feel welcome from day one.

👟
Consistent Presence

Show up most weeks. Arrive early, linger after. Be visibly approachable. That alone changes how new runners experience the group.

💬
Community Builders

Spark conversations, celebrate milestones, encourage post-run hangouts. Help turn a run into a community.

📣
Honest Voices

Share real feedback through quarterly surveys. Tell us what's working, what's not, and what newcomers are experiencing.

Core Expectations

01
Welcome newcomers

Greet at least one new runner each week. Introduce them to someone else. Help first-timers understand where to stand, how regrouping works, and what to expect.

02
Be present & approachable

Attend runs regularly. Most weeks, life acknowledged. Arrive early, stay late. Your visible presence is the role.

03
Model the culture

Use inclusive, encouraging language. Reinforce "community first, pace second." Run different distances to avoid pace silos. Be active, not passive.

04
Build post-run energy

Attend post-run hangouts occasionally. Help normalize gathering together instead of dispersing the moment the run ends.
